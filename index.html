Visi PT. Tirta Purbalingga Adijaya

To become the best plastic packaging manufacturer in ASEAN by providing quality products developed together with customers under medium to long term contracts to a cross section of selected end markets at the lowest possible production cost.

Misi PT. Tirta Purbalingga Adijaya

  1. To produce quality products at a competitive price meeting customers stringent product specifications and quality requirements within shortest possible delivery time.
  2. To be the forefront leader in production technology and innovation achieving market leadership reputable and internationally recognized brand names within high growth industries.
  3. To develop production lines at customer’s premises for a longer term contract period to serve customers better in terms of reduced delivery time, highest possible quality and eliminating the need for customers to maintain high inventories. (In plant concept).
